Detailed Description

Parse in .obj's.

I could do more parsers for other formats but .objs fit all my needs for now (I'll need my own format most likely). Perhaps I should further separate obj loading from the physics operations for finding the interior points and so forth. Probably this shouldn't be a movable subclass anymore.
