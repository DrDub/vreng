#define VJC_HDR_SSRC_IDX   3

Packet header.

WARNING !!! If you update this structure, do not forget to update the VJC_HDR_LEN, VJC_HDR_SSRC_IDX and VJC_HDR_DATALEN_IDX. VJC_HDR_LEN is the total length (in 8bit ints, no padding) of this structure, and VJC_HDR_DATALEN_IDX is the position of the 16bit data length field within the structure. VJC_HDR_SSRC_IDX is the same, but to the 32bit app_ssrc field.
