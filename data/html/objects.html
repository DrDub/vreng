mech monster walking

fountain to do a fountain effect
rain to do raining effect
snow to do snowing effect
bubble to see textual chat above the head
terrain to make different terrains
river to make different rivers
sun to light scene according to hours
cloth to expose different clothes
halo to wear over the head
hat to wear over the head
dress to wear over the body
wings to wear over the back
fire fire effect
haid to apply upon the body
hairs to apply upon the head
slider to attract avatar
bumper to repulse avatar
